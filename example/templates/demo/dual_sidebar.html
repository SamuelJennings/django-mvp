About This Layout

This configuration shows the full power of the inner layout system with both primary (left) and secondary (right) sidebars active. The primary sidebar provides navigation and filters, while the secondary sidebar shows metadata and related content.

Use cases: Complex dashboards, detailed analytics, rich content management interfaces.

Section 1: Overview

The dual sidebar layout is ideal for applications that need to present a lot of information while maintaining organization. Both sidebars are independently collapsible, allowing users to customize their view based on their current task.
